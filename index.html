Our Story

A neighborhood cafe with heart

Mochi Mochi Cafe is a family owned and operated coffee shop in Fort Lee, NJ. We opened in 2022 with one goal — to create a warm, welcoming space where everyone feels at home.

From hand-crafted lattes and bubble teas to Korean bingsu and freshly made croffles, every item is made with care. Newly renovated with a study-friendly environment perfect for casual meetups too.
